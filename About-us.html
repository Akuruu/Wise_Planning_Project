Our purpose and goal


App Description

Our idea is to create a clean and simple website that will assist users in finding stock market information from different sources within just one search. The website will have a search tool where users will be able to search market data and articles regarding a specific company in the stock market. In order to create our vision, we would need a search tool that will ask for the company name or stock symbol and the search will return stock market data and related news.


Simplicity

Tired of not knowing where to look? Too much information to keep up with? Us at Wise Planning are trying to give users a simple, easy to navigate interface that allows you to see what you want to see when you want to see it with minimal noise.

Stocks At Your Fingertips

We want to bring you the stocks you're trying to get more information from straight to you. By providing you with reccomended news and info about the companies you're interested in finding out about more at the click of a mouse.

Beginner Friendly

Even if you're just getting into stocks for the first time and don't know where to start, we're with you! We want to give YOU the ability to comfortably narrow your search results down and provide vital information that's easy to understand.
